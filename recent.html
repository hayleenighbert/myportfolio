UX Challenges

Building a tool that will work for everyone

  • Tackling a complex CMS may seem daunting, but our team was able to address usability concerns and do research to cut down on the application's complexity by at least 50%.
  • As an internal-facing tool, it needed to be fully accessible because Microsoft is an inclusive work environment. Designers, project managers, marketing teams, and development teams need to use the product regardless of ability. That is why it is important to follow W3C accessibility standards across every aspect of the application.
  • One of the biggest challenges we faced was editing from a UI development level. The tool certainly didn't lack features. However, there wasn't a clear path to access those important features. By addressing bugs, usability concerns, and human-computer interaction challenges, the team was better prepared for launch.
  • Whiteboarding ideation was common practice on our team. We tried to get into the mind of the user and how they would navigate the app without frustration or cognitive overload. "How will the user get from point A to point B, and how quickly will they do it?" Ask a lot of questions.
  • As both a UX Designer and Web Developer, I was able to facilitate between teams to try and address these issues with usability and accessibility. Forms must be easy-to-use, navigation simple and intuitive, and features simplified and categorized by importance.

Accessibility

Usable for Everyone

Beyond point-and-click usability, accessibility ensures users of any ability can have a good user experience and an easy way to access necessary information with tools like tab order and screen readers.

Why is accessibility important in software?

  • Accessibility principles provide ways for potential users, regardless of ability, to navigate software with ease and still have a good user experience.
  • Using screen readers and specific code snippets, software can become even more alive and tangible for all users.
  • Accessibility is extremely important for good software. Best practices should be implemented throughout the design process, especially for internal-facing company tools.
